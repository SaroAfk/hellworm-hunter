Privacy Policy

App: Hellworm Hunter (Overwolf app for Diablo IV)

Summary

Hellworm Hunter is an in-game overlay that shows you where the next Helltide spawn is in Diablo IV. It is designed to work with the least data possible. It does not collect your name, email, or Diablo/Battle.net account, and it does not record or transmit your gameplay.

Game data (read locally, not transmitted)

The app reads real-time information from Diablo IV through the Overwolf Game Events platform — specifically your in-game position and the current zone/region. This data is used only on your own device, in real time, to draw the on-screen arrow, place map markers, and plan routes. It is not stored on our servers and is not transmitted off your machine.

Data the app sends to our server

To manage purchases and the free trial, the app contacts the developer's licensing endpoint (a Cloudflare Worker). It sends:

  1. License key — a random, non-personal code generated on your device the first time you launch the app. It is not linked to your identity. It is used to check which styles/features you have purchased.
  2. Trial fingerprint — a one-way hash derived from hardware characteristics (operating system, CPU, motherboard serial, memory). Only the hash is sent; the underlying hardware values are not transmitted and the hash cannot be reversed to identify you. It exists solely to enforce the one-hour free trial and prevent it from being reset.

As with any internet request, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may process your IP address to route the request; we do not use it to build a profile.

Purchases

Purchases are handled by our storefront provider, Tebex. When you buy, the app opens the Tebex store in your browser. Payment and billing information is handled by Tebex, not by this app. See Tebex's own privacy policy.

Data stored on your device

Your settings, chosen styles, license key, and trial start time are stored locally in the app's storage on your PC. Clearing the app's data removes them.

What we do NOT collect

  • No name, email, or account credentials.
  • No screenshots, video, or recordings of your gameplay leave your machine.
  • No chat, no keystroke logging, no location data outside the game.

The Overwolf platform

The app runs on Overwolf. Overwolf's own client collects data under Overwolf's Privacy Policy, which applies in addition to this policy.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from them.

Changes
