Hellwyrm Compass

Stop circling the Helltide. A quiet overlay arrow that always knows where the next Hellwyrm spawn is — and the fastest order to run them.

Free

The compass

A single click-through arrow beside your character pointing to the nearest known Hellwyrm spawn, with live distance. Appears during active Helltides, stays out of your way otherwise.

Free

Live zone map

A small always-on-top zone map with every community-sourced spawn marked. Markers flip as you clear them and re-arm on the respawn cycle, so you always know what's left.

One-time unlock

Route planner

Click spawns in the order you want to run them and the compass walks you through the circuit, looping until the Helltide ends. Save named routes per zone and reload them next time. One purchase, yours forever — no subscription.

Built the right way

Hellwyrm Compass reads only Overwolf's official Diablo IV game events to know where you are. No memory reading, no input automation, no game-file access — just a rendered overlay and public community spawn data. Purely informational, fully within Overwolf's game compliance principles.
